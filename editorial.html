Gratitude to the armed forces

Gratitude cannot be forced, it can only be felt As India crossed her 75 years of Independence in August 2021, there was gratitude which remained to be shown to the Indian Armed Forces for their immense contribution throughout the years to our Motherland. Hence, The Student Council at AMS P Obul Reddy Public School took pride in launching the *Desh Ka Rakhwaala* Initiative where we requested each individual/team/department to come forward and submit letters of gratitude and small artwroks to the saviours, which were in turn sent by postal mail to Udhampur Cantonment, Line of Control, Indo-Pak Border, Rajasthan, India. The initiative was a huge success receiving a total of 200+ responses from students and Management.
