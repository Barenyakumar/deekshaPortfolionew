The Desh Ka Rakhwaala initiative

Gratitude to the armed forces

As India crossed her 75 years of Independence in August 2021, there was gratitude which remained to be shown to the Indian Armed Forces for their immense contribution throughout the years to our Motherland. Hence, The Student Council at AMS P Obul Reddy Public School took pride in launching the *Desh Ka Rakhwaala* Initiative where we requested each individual/team/department to come forward and submit letters of gratitude and small artwroks to the saviours, which were in turn sent by postal mail to Udhampur Cantonment, Line of Control, Indo-Pak Border, Rajasthan, India. The initiative was a huge success receiving a total of 200+ responses from students and Management.

A filtering team of our board of student editors tuned and handwrote the received letters. Meetings were carried out to distribute tasks and allocate responsibilities for the responses we received. I led the team for voluntary positions offering students opportunities to harness their skills
• Handwriter Duty: Handwriting all the letters in doc/other digital formats
• Proof-Reader Duty: Checking all letters and rejecting ones with a negative emotion or too many mistakes
• Formatter Duty: Covert all kinds of formats into PDFs.
