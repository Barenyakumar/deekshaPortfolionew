The Metaverse

Research

Life and beyond

The successor to the mobile internet” as the world receives it, VR systems are an era of technological evolution. “I am proud to announce that starting today, our company is now Meta….”, the inverted eight curled into infinity as CEO Zuckerberg announced Facebook’s rebranding- it is surreal as people’s lifestyles bore a new face in the virtual world. Experience, Expression, Transportation, knowledge, Productivity and the Economy were utterly moved into a new dimension altogether; One that doesn’t exist in the real world amongst real people. The research was an eye-opener that the very essence of existence is soon to be wiped off; the world will no longer be functioning, and all people need is a headset, a seat and the Internet. A source that stood out was a cover story by “The Week”- it took readers into the meta world and presented before their eyes, the repercussions of it. My teammates and I had to introduce the world of the metaverse and the impact that would befall if it were to become a part of our lives. My research gave me an insight into the importance of moderation; While the exploitation of social media through Abusive language, inappropriate posts, and hate speech is widespread, a titanic shift as the Metaverse where the entire time is spent in a non-existent digital world is going to make supervision of abuse an arduous task. An interest to delve deeper was enhanced as I encountered the perils; I wanted to bring into the limelight that the Metaverse isn’t something in the making, it is here on platforms like Roblox and Fortnite seeping more and more into people’s lives.
